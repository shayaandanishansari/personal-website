Pricing

This website uses a project-support pricing model for software projects published here.

How pricing works

  • Payments are optional and are made in connection with a selected software project on this website.
  • There are no fixed prices. Where enabled, you can choose an amount within the available options.
  • Payments support the development, maintenance, hosting, and availability of software projects.

What a payment represents

A payment made here is a software-related transaction tied to a project.
Because software projects evolve over time, payments do not guarantee specific features, timelines, outcomes, or ongoing availability.

Payment processing

Payments are processed securely by Paddle, which acts as the merchant of record.

Refunds

Payments are final and non-refundable, except where required by applicable law or Paddle’s policies.
