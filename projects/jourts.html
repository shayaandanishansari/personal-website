JOURTS

A gourmet food e-commerce platform with real-time inventory

Overview

JOURTS is a full-stack e-commerce platform built for gourmet and artisan food brands. The focus was on building a shopping experience that feels premium — fast load times, smooth transitions, and a checkout flow with zero friction.

The platform features real-time inventory tracking so customers never encounter out-of-stock surprises at checkout, personalised product recommendations based on browsing history, and seamless Stripe-powered payments with saved card support.

On the backend, an admin dashboard gives store owners full control over product listings, orders, and customer data, with CSV export for accounting and fulfilment workflows.
