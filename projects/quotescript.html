QuoteScript

CRM-integrated quote generation and management automation

Overview

QuoteScript automates the most tedious part of the sales process — building, formatting, and tracking quotes. It connects directly to your CRM, pulls in client and product data, and generates professionally formatted quotes in seconds using customisable templates.

The template engine supports dynamic variable injection, conditional sections, and branded styling. Generated quotes are delivered as PDF or shareable web links, with read receipts so you know exactly when a client has opened their quote.

A lightweight dashboard tracks quote status across your pipeline — sent, opened, accepted, declined — and integrates with common CRM platforms via REST API and webhook support.
